Recommended Resources

Books

Note: Some of the books are country-specific, in which case I’ve indicated this in the description.
Most of the learnings and insights from the US-focused books are universally relevant, but product recommendations and investment strategies are focused for that country and not necessarily applicable or available to people living elsewhere.

The Simple Path to Wealth

by J.L. Collins

Great inspirational book that is one of the most clearest and easily understandable books on investing for wealth generation.

€24.75

Balance

by Andrew Hallam

Andrew Hallam is an investment guru that inspires with his clear and funny writing on how to achieve financial freedom.

€16.99

Millionaire Expat

by Andrew Hallam

Hallam’s book that focuses on helping expats navigate financial challenges and build wealth. Includes also some specific advice for particular nationalities.

€19.99

I Will Teach You To Be Rich

by Ramit Sethi

Book that focuses how to get finances under control, including managing credit cards, bank accounts, setting up retirement savings, conscious spending and even how to have money discussions with your partner.

€16.39

Mastering the Market Cycle

by Howard Marks

Book that focuses how to get finances under control, including managing credit cards, bank accounts, setting up retirement savings, conscious spending and even how to have money discussions with your partner.

€18.66

Podcasts

I Will Teach You To Be Rich

Entertaining podcast where couples discuss their financial situation with financial coach Ramit Sethi.
Sprinkled with good advice and delves on the money mindset and emotional context in relationships.
US-based but lessons are applicable anywhere.

The Wallet

Podcast by Emily Bellet, focusing on different topics that are relevant for women from inheritance, taxes, investing, and beyond, including interviews with experts.

Economist Money Talks

Interesting in-depth reportage of key current financial and investing themes.

The Bid

Podcast from world’s biggest Asset Manager BlackRock focuses on investment themes, market outlook, retirement and various other investment-related topics.

The Memo by Howard Marks

Insightful observations on how markets work and how investors’ psychology influences the state of the markets.
For those who are interested in understanding the cyclical phenomena that repeat regularly in the markets and gaining an advantage for investing.

Websites

The Poor Swiss

Swiss-specific blogger who provides valuable tips, reviews, recommendations and useful articles on investing, retirement, real-estate purchase etc. Focused on Switzerland.

Marmot.Finance

Swiss-based investment manager focusing on empowering and inspiring women on investing. Marmot organizes regularly free webinars and has tools (e.g., Money Makeover Quiz) available on their website.

CNN Greed & Fear Indicator

This is an indicator that shows the sentiment of the financial markets at any given moment, using multiple data points. When markets are in “Greed” mode, stock prices are generally going up and investors are in an exuberant buying mode. When markets are in “Fear” mode, stock prices are generally going down and investors are more fearful of losses and in selling mode.
